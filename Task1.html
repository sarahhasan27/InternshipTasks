The Midnight Library

Matt Haig

The Midnight Library is is a compelling and thought-provoking novel that explores themes of regret, second chances, and the pursuit of happiness. It offers a unique and imaginative concept where the protagonist, Nora Seed, finds herself in a mysterious library that contains infinite books representing different versions of her life. Each book represents a different decision she could have made and offers the opportunity to explore alternate paths.

Little Women

The novel explores themes of sisterhood, love, friendship, and the pursuit of dreams, making it a relatable and engaging read.
